Strategic Plan 2025–2027

Laying the Cornerstones of Integrity-Driven Leadership

Overview

The 2025–2027 Strategic Focus Plan represents the foundational phase of The Conservatives. This period is dedicated to building strong governance systems, launching leadership programs, establishing infrastructure, and creating sustainable partnerships.

Strategic Objectives

1. Strengthen Governance & Leadership

Establish accountable leadership structures, finalize the constitution, and operationalize transparent decision-making systems.

2. Launch Certificate Programs

Develop and pilot leadership and governance certificate programs aligned with national and international standards.

3. Digital & Physical Infrastructure

Build the institutional platforms, systems, and spaces that support learning, coordination, and growth.

4. Partnerships & Visibility

Form strategic collaborations and position The Conservatives as a credible thought-leadership institution.

5. Resource Mobilization

Establish sustainable financing, volunteer networks, and transparent financial management systems.

Key Milestones

2025

Constitution ratified and Interim Executive Committee installed.

2026

Pilot certificate programs launched and virtual learning platforms initiated.

2027

First Leadership Forum hosted and accreditation processes underway.
