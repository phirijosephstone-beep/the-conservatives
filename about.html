Our Story

The Conservatives was founded as a response to the growing need for ethical leadership, constitutional literacy, and principled governance across Africa. The institution exists to cultivate leaders who are grounded in integrity, accountability, and service to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading African institution that inspires and develops ethical, visionary leaders who transform governance systems and communities.

Our Mission

To educate, equip, and empower current and emerging leaders through governance education, leadership training, and service-driven innovation.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, dignity, accountability, inclusive growth, and commitment to justice.
